# PiLearn (Pi Research Labs) – Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pi Research Labs (“we”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the PiLearn mobile application and related services.

## 1. Information we collect

- **Account and profile:** name, email address, and (if you provide) phone number, user type (student or professional), university or company, course, year of study, city, state, designation.
- **Usage and engagement:** events you attend, materials you download, polls you answer, courses you view or register for, and interest/registration status.
- **Device and app:** we may collect device type and app usage data necessary to operate and improve the app.

## 2. How we use your information

- To provide and improve the PiLearn platform (courses, events, certifications, and learning materials).
- To communicate with you about courses, events, and support.
- To run analytics, leaderboards, and gamification features.
- To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

## 3. Sharing of information

- We may share information with partner institutions and third-party certification providers when necessary to deliver courses and issue certificates.
- We do not sell your personal information. We may share aggregated or anonymized data for research or improvement purposes.

## 4. Data retention and security

- We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services and comply with law.
- We use industry-standard measures to protect your data; no system is completely secure.

## 5. Your rights

- You may access, update, or delete your profile from the app.
- You may withdraw consent or request data export where applicable by law.
- Contact us (see below) to exercise these rights.

## 6. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact Pi Research Labs at the contact details provided in the app or on our website.

By using the PiLearn app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
